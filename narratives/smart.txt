Kirby Smart has built one of the fastest-growing coaching trees in college football since taking over at Georgia in 2016. His tree spans 989 coaches across four degrees of separation, including 55 current or former FBS head coaches.
Eight coaches who served directly on Smart's staff went on to become FBS head coaches: Dan Lanning, Dell McGee, Fran Brown, Jeremy Pruitt, Mel Tucker, Mike Bobo, Sam Pittman, and Shane Beamer. Each went on to become an FBS head coach after serving on his staff.
The depth 2 branch is still early but already compounding. Shane Beamer's branch produced Dowell Loggains and Shawn Elliott. Chris Rumph's branch added Tim Skipper.
At depth 2 there are 144 coaches connected to Smart's tree. By depth 4 that number reaches 542.
Smart's tree is notable for how quickly it has grown relative to his tenure length — eight depth 1 HC mentees in under a decade at Georgia places him among the most productive staff pipelines in the current era.
